How Recommendations Work
The engine uses quiz score, task completion, and self-reported difficulty for each session.
Two consecutive scores below 60% with DIFFICULT self-ratings: Step Down one level.
Score above 80% with an EASY self-rating: Step Up one level.
All other signal combinations: Maintain current level.
Teachers can confirm the recommendation or override it before applying.
